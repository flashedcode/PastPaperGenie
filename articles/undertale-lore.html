Undertale Lore Explained

A complete breakdown of Undertale and Undertale Yellow storylines, characters, and secrets.

What is Undertale?

Undertale is an RPG where players control a human who falls into the Underground, a world filled with monsters, choices, and multiple story paths.

Core Story Paths

  • Neutral Route – mixed choices and outcomes
  • Pacifist Route – spare all monsters
  • Genocide Route – defeat all enemies

Each route changes the story, dialogue, and ending completely.

Main Characters

  • Frisk – the player character
  • Sans – mysterious skeleton with hidden power
  • Papyrus – optimistic and comedic skeleton
  • Toriel – caring guardian of the Ruins
  • Asgore – ruler of the Underground
  • Flowey – unsettling entity manipulating the world

Undertale Yellow

Undertale Yellow is a fan-made expansion that explores new characters, storylines, and areas within the Undertale universe.

It adds depth to the world and expands on the original game's lore.

Why Undertale is So Popular

Undertale became famous for its emotional storytelling, unique combat system, and meaningful player choices that affect the entire game world.
