What is Friday Night Funkin’?

Friday Night Funkin’ (FNF) is a rhythm-based music game where players must match arrows to the beat in music battles against opponents.

How to Play FNF

  • Use arrow keys to match the rhythm
  • Press keys in time with the music
  • Win battles by outperforming your opponent
  • Progress through increasingly difficult songs

Why FNF is So Popular

FNF became viral due to its catchy music, simple controls, and massive modding community.

New mods constantly add characters, songs, and challenges that keep the game fresh in 2026.

FNF Mods & Community

The FNF community has created thousands of mods featuring custom songs, characters, and storylines.

This makes FNF one of the most replayable rhythm games online.
