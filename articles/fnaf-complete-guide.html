What is Five Nights at Freddy’s?

Five Nights at Freddy’s (FNAF) is a survival horror game series where players must survive nights inside haunted locations filled with animatronics.

Gameplay Overview

The core gameplay focuses on surviving nights by managing limited resources, watching security cameras, and preventing animatronics from reaching you.

Each game introduces new mechanics, difficulty levels, and horror elements.

Why FNAF is So Popular

FNAF became a global phenomenon due to its unique horror style, hidden lore, and intense jump-scare mechanics.

Even in 2026, the series remains one of the most searched horror franchises online.
