# Fallacious0

## 🎮 About Fallacious0

Fallacious0 is a school-friendly gaming website built for students who want fun, fast, and easy games to play during breaks, free periods, or after finishing work. The goal of the site is to provide entertaining browser games that are simple to access and enjoyable for everyone.

---

## ✨ Features

* Fast and easy-to-play browser games
* Simple website design
* Works on most school devices
* No complicated downloads
* Constantly updated with new games
* Fun games for different interests and skill levels

---

## 🚀 Purpose

The purpose of Fallacious0 is to create a fun online space where students can relax, challenge themselves, and enjoy games with friends during school free time.
